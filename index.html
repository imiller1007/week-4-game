BOMB DEFUSAL


1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. There are four buttons below. By clicking on a button you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

3.You defuse the bomb by matching your total score to the random number, the bomb detonates if your total score goes above the random number.

4. The value of each button is hidden from you until you click on it.

5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each button.
